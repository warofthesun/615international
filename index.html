TechnologyAdvice

TechnologyAdvice prides itself in helping B2B tech buyers manage the complexity and risk of the buying process. We are a trusted source of information for tech buyers, delivering advice and facilitating connections between our buyers and the world’s leading sellers of business technology.

Established in 2006 out of our founder’s dorm room, we continue to expand our business. In 2021 we acquired TechRepublic, and in 2020, we acquired QuinStreet B2B, both leading resources for IT and business decision-makers. These critical acquisitions included global teams of dynamic technology and media professionals to enhance our presence in the US, the UK, Singapore, and Australia. We now have more than 20 digital media brands and over 500 global team members, representing 12 languages.

      Woolworth Theatre

      Woolworth Theatre aims to provide a unique, first-of-its-kind theatre experience in downtown Nashville. The 615 Group has teamed up with Country Music Artist, Chuck Wicks, to bring the concept to life.

      The venue will house Nashville’s first theatrical stage residency which is written by Chuck Wicks and Two-Time Emmy Winners, NappyTabs. The Woolworth Theatre will have a reimagined cocktail lounge in the basement. The main theatre and cocktail lounge will also be available as event spaces.

      Philanthropy

      • Best Buddies

        Whether we are dressing up for a gala or dressing down for a run, we support Best Buddies in a variety of ways. Some of our favorite events include their annual Best Buddies Gala and the local Best Buddies 5k.

      • Second Harvest

        We’ve supported Second Harvest annually since 2014, holding can food competitions and collecting more than 1,600 pounds of food in a single month. In 2017, our entire team participated in the annual Martin Luther King Jr. Day Sort-a-thon and contributed to the more than 80,000 meals that were sorted.

      • Nashville Food Project

        Working to alleviate hunger in our city, the Nashville Food Project grows and collects food to distribute meals to those who need it most. We enjoyed preparing vegetables and making kale chips.

      • Nashville Youth Villages

        For the past three years, we’ve supported Youth Villages’ Holiday Heroes initiative and have shopped and bought more than 300 presents for children each year. We also participate in Backpack Heroes to donate school supplies to Nashville children.

      • St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital

        The mission of St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital is to advance cures, and means of prevention, for pediatric catastrophic diseases through research and treatment.

      • Hollis Adams Foundation

        The Hollis Adams Foundation mission is to open the world to adults with developmental disabilities in central Indiana.

      • Safe Haven

        Safe Haven has 30 years of experience in the changing field of family homelessness with proactive programs, evidence based practices and trauma informed care.

      • Gigi’s Playhouse

        GiGi’s Playhouse Down Syndrome Achievement Center’s mission is empower individuals with Down syndrome, their families and the community
